Expert Health Club Cleaning Services Since 1979

Crystal Building Maintenance has been providing specialized cleaning services to health clubs, gyms, and fitness centers throughout Palm Beach County for over 45 years. We understand the unique challenges of maintaining a clean, hygienic environment in high-traffic fitness facilities where member health and satisfaction are paramount.

Comprehensive Fitness Facility Cleaning

Health clubs require meticulous attention to cleanliness and sanitation. Our experienced team is trained in the specific requirements of fitness facilities, including proper disinfection protocols for exercise equipment, locker rooms, showers, saunas, and pool areas. We use EPA-approved disinfectants and follow CDC guidelines to ensure a safe, healthy environment for your members.

While many companies base their fees on square footage alone, our fees are based upon a developed quoting program using time study analysis. This method accounts for the intensive cleaning requirements of fitness facilities, including equipment sanitization, high-traffic areas, and specialized spaces like studios and pool decks.

Quality Assurance for Your Fitness Facility

Crystal Building Maintenance provides personal customer care contact to ensure your satisfaction. An experienced supervisor oversees each job site, and a Customer Relations/Quality Assurance Inspector regularly visits to ensure expectations are met or exceeded, addressing the unique needs of health club environments.
