Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond for emergency cleaning?

We offer 24/7 emergency service across Palm Beach County. For urgent cleaning like flood damage or facility sanitization, we typically respond within 2-4 hours. Same-day service is available for most emergency situations.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all commercial cleaning projects. We'll assess your facility, discuss options, and provide a detailed written estimate at no cost.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed and insured with comprehensive coverage. We've been serving Palm Beach County since 1979, ensuring professional craftsmanship on every project.

What areas do you serve in Palm Beach County?

We serve all of Palm Beach County including West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, Jupiter, Wellington, Lake Worth, Palm Beach Gardens, and surrounding areas.

Do you offer green cleaning options?

Yes, we offer environmentally friendly cleaning chemicals and techniques upon request. Our green cleaning methods are safe for employees while maintaining the highest cleaning standards.

Do you clean up after the job is complete?

Absolutely. We believe in leaving your property cleaner than we found it. All equipment is properly stored, surfaces are restored, and we do a thorough cleanup before considering any job complete.
