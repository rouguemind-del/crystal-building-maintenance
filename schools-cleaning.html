Jessica Lunsford Act Compliance

All CBM staff assigned to educational facilities undergo mandatory Level 2 FDLE/FBI background screenings in full compliance with the Jessica Lunsford Act. The safety of your students is our absolute priority.

What We Clean

  • Classrooms and instructional spaces
  • Restrooms and high-traffic corridors
  • Cafeterias and food service areas
  • Gymnasiums and locker rooms
  • Administrative offices
  • Nurseries and early childhood areas (child-safe products)

Green Cleaning Options

We offer environmentally friendly, non-toxic cleaning chemicals and techniques upon request — ideal for schools prioritizing student and staff health.

Quality Assurance

An experienced supervisor oversees each job site following a daily work schedule. A Customer Relations/Quality Assurance Inspector visits to meet with the facility manager during office hours to ensure expectations are met or exceeded.
